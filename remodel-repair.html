Pool Remodel & Repair

Over time, swimming pools can become worn out and in need of repair. We provide repair & replacement services on pool equipment, electrical systems, decking, plaster, automated controllers and much more. If you feel like your backyard is in need of a outdoor face-lift, we encourage you to give us a call and schedule a free estimate so that you can take your next step towards refreshing your backyard landscape.

We also specialize in Remodel & Renovation Services to boost your backyard performance into the new generation of pool automated controllers or asthetic appearance. Now-a-days, it's standard for pools to be built with LED Lighting, iPhone Controls, In-Floor Cleaning Systems, Robotic Cleaners and Travertine Decking. At Titus County Pools and Spas, we provide a one-stop shop for all backyard Renovation Services.
