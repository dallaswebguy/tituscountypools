Gunite Shell Construction

Building a Gunite (Concrete) Pool is similar to building a house - it requires creating the "frame work" of interlocking steel and reinforcing it with concrete and pressure. We prefer to provide surplus materials to ensure that your shell lasts longer. It requires more effort but your pool shell with last a lot longer!

Excavation

The first step in any pool construction is digging the hole where the pool with lay. This is designed during our "creation" phase where we get your ideas on how you want your pool laid out.

Rebar

Tying the Steel (rebar) is what provides strength and durability for your pool. We always dig another 4" deep and use 1/2" steel rather than the standard 1/4" steel. This provides us additional strength that other pool companies don't offer.
